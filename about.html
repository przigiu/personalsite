05_ABOUT ME

I'm Giulia Peruzzi, a UI/UX designer with a passion for creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. I believe design should solve problems while bringing joy to users.

BACKGROUND

With over 5 years of experience in marketing and creative direction at D2C and B2B companies, I've built a foundation in connecting people to products through strategic storytelling and visuals. My transition into UX/UI design bridges brand thinking with user experience, allowing me to create solutions that are both intentionally crafted and business-minded.

APPROACH

I believe beauty in function isn't a luxury, it's essential. I approach every project with intention and attention to detail. Understanding how design shapes experience allows me to craft solutions that are not only beautiful but also functional. My marketing background gives me a strategic lens for balancing user needs with business goals, creating cohesive touch points across the entire journey.

EXPERTISE

UX/UI Design Visual Design Brand Strategy Creative Direction User Journey Content Strategy Design Systems E-commerce Systems

BEYOND WORK

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring architecture, experimenting with graphics, or searching for the perfect cup of tea. I believe that inspiration comes from everywhere, and these pursuits often influence my design thinking.
